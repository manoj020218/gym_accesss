The Product

SmartGym EDGE Platform

A complete gym management ecosystem — from the door to the dashboard, from the edge device to the cloud.

🖥️
Web Admin Dashboard
Full-featured React PWA for managing members, staff, memberships, payments, products, and detailed reports. Works on any browser.
📱
Member Mobile App
Flutter app for gym members — digital membership card, QR code access, workout tracking, renewal reminders, and push notifications.
⚡
Edge Service
Runs on a mini-PC at each gym branch. Makes access control decisions locally in milliseconds — fully offline-capable with 72-hour sync buffer.
🎭
Face Recognition
U5 biometric hardware integration for touchless face-recognition entry. Templates stored on-device, never transmitted to cloud.
📡
RFID & QR Access
Supports RFID cards, QR codes, and face recognition simultaneously. Compatible with standard Wiegand and U5 hardware controllers.
☁️
Cloud + On-Premise
Flexible deployment — self-hosted VPS for the cloud layer, your own hardware for the edge. No vendor lock-in. Full data portability.
Who We Are

Built by Practitioners

Jenix India is a technology firm focused on IoT and software solutions for the fitness and security industries. We have hands-on experience deploying access control systems in real gyms, which means we've felt the pain of systems that go offline at the worst time, hardware that doesn't integrate, and reporting that doesn't match what owners actually need.

SmartGym EDGE is the system we wished existed. We built it with Node.js, React, MongoDB, and Flutter — proven, open-technology stack — so it's maintainable, extensible, and not dependent on any single vendor ecosystem.
