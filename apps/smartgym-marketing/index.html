Features

Everything Your Gym Needs

A complete, production-ready access control platform — not just software, a full ecosystem.

RFID & QR Access

Supports Wiegand 26/34-bit RFID readers, QR token scanning, and TCP-connected biometric devices (ZKTeco and compatible). One door controller handles them all.

Multi-Branch Cloud

Centralized web admin dashboard to manage every branch, every member, and every door — from a single login. Real-time access logs synced to the cloud.

Offline-First Edge

Raspberry Pi edge service runs locally at each branch. If internet goes down, access decisions are made locally. Fail-closed security means the door stays locked for unknowns.

Member Mobile App

Expo React Native app for Android & iOS. Members see their QR card, access history, active plan, and get instant push notifications for entries and plan expiry.

Smart Billing

Manage member plans, track payments, and send automated renewal reminders. Full payment history, outstanding balances, and plan lifecycle management built in.

Reports & Analytics

Peak hour analysis, revenue trends, member retention, attendance heatmaps, and branch comparison reports — all in the web dashboard.

Push Notifications

Firebase FCM push notifications to member phones for access events, plan expiry warnings, and custom gym announcements. Multi-topic broadcast support.

Staff Management

Role-based admin access. Branch managers see only their branch. Head office sees everything. Staff clock-in logs, access event audit trails, and activity reports.

Open REST API

Documented RESTful API for every feature. Integrate your POS, ERP, or third-party app. Webhook support for real-time event streaming to external systems.

Process

Up and Running in 3 Steps

No complex IT setup. No expensive hardware lock-in. Deploy in a day.

1

Install Edge Device

Mount a Raspberry Pi or mini PC at each branch. Connect your RFID reader, relay, LEDs, and buzzer. Flash our edge service — done in under 30 minutes.

2

Configure & Add Members

Open the web admin, add your branch, enroll members with their RFID card numbers or assign QR tokens. Set plans and access zones — no technical expertise needed.

3

Go Live Instantly

Members scan their card or show their QR. The door opens in under 50ms. Every entry is logged in real time. Members get instant push notifications on their phone.
